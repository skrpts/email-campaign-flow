## Email Marketing Playbook

### Campaign Types

| Type | Frequency | Objective |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Newsletter | Weekly/Fortnightly | Engagement, brand awareness |
| Promotional | As needed | Conversion, revenue |
| Transactional | Triggered | Confirmation, onboarding |
| Re-engagement | Quarterly | Win-back inactive subscribers |

### Best Practices

#### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use personalisation where appropriate
- Create urgency without being clickbait
- A/B test every campaign

#### Send Timing
- Test send times for your specific audience
- Generally: Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm
- Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons

#### Design
- Mobile-first design (60%+ opens on mobile)
- Single column layout
- Clear visual hierarchy
- One primary CTA above the fold

### Metrics to Track

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| Open rate | Subject line effectiveness |
| Click-through rate | Content relevance |
| Conversion rate | Offer and CTA effectiveness |
| Unsubscribe rate | List health and content alignment |
| Bounce rate | List quality |
