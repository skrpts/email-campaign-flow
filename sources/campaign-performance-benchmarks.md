## Overview

Industry benchmark data for evaluating email campaign performance against standard metrics.

## Email Marketing Benchmarks

### Open Rates
| Industry | Average Open Rate |
|----------|:-----------------:|
| Technology | 22% |
| E-commerce | 18% |
| Education | 28% |
| Non-profit | 26% |
| Overall average | 21% |

### Click-Through Rates
| Industry | Average CTR |
|----------|:-----------:|
| Technology | 3.2% |
| E-commerce | 2.8% |
| Education | 4.1% |
| Non-profit | 3.6% |
| Overall average | 3.0% |

### Other Metrics
- Average bounce rate: 0.5%
- Average unsubscribe rate: 0.2%
- Average conversion rate: 1.5%

## Interpretation Guide

- Above benchmark: Campaign performing well, optimise further
- At benchmark: Acceptable performance, test for improvements
- Below benchmark: Investigate issues (list quality, content relevance, send timing)
