## Overview

Brand voice guidelines ensure all email communications maintain a consistent tone and personality.

## Voice Attributes

### Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Confident but not pushy
- Helpful and solution-oriented

### Email-Specific Rules
- Subject lines: Benefit-led, under 50 characters
- Preview text: Complement (don't repeat) the subject line
- Body: Conversational, scannable, mobile-friendly
- CTAs: Action-oriented, specific, single primary CTA per email
