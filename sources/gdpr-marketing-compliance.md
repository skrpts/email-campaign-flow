## Overview

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) compliance requirements for email marketing activities.

## Key Requirements

### Consent
- Explicit opt-in required for marketing emails
- Pre-ticked boxes are not valid consent
- Consent must be freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous
- Record when and how consent was obtained

### Right to Withdraw
- Every email must include an unsubscribe mechanism
- Unsubscribe must be honoured within 30 days (best practice: immediately)
- Make unsubscribe process simple (one click preferred)

### Data Usage
- Only process data for the stated purpose
- Do not share subscriber data without consent
- Maintain up-to-date privacy policy

### Record Keeping
- Keep records of consent
- Document data processing activities
- Be prepared to demonstrate compliance

## Email Marketing Checklist

- [ ] Valid consent obtained for all recipients
- [ ] Unsubscribe link included
- [ ] Sender clearly identified
- [ ] Physical address included
- [ ] Privacy policy linked
- [ ] Data used only for stated purpose
